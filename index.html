Name:

Pocket LandLord

Function:

The Application helps bridge the gap between the landlord, the tenant and the bank. 20/11/2019

Contribution by:
  • Elizabeth Otieno
  • Stephen Kimanthi

Description

The Pocket Landlord is very convenient. The house owner or the landlord registers for the app and is given administrative rights. The landlord then inputs details of all their tenants. The tenants then get notifications on their emails to accept the invite and they all receive unique codes (IDs). From there the tenants can be able to see thir payment history, their payment modes and they get notifications when the rent is due. Once they pay thier rent they get an electronic receipt through their emails.

Set up requirements

  • A smart phone or laptop
  • Reliable internet connection
  • All pertinent tenant information

This application needs only a one time installation on your machine, if there are any updates for the app, we do them OTA, which does not inconvenience the clients at any point during the interaction with the app.The App does not currentle depend on any other code besides the one we used to create it.
